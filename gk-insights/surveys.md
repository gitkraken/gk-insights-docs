## Overview

GitKraken Insights Surveys lets you send pre-built questionnaires to developers on your team. Survey results integrate with Insights metrics and industry benchmarks to surface actionable recommendations, combining code activity data with developer sentiment.

---

## Prerequisites

- An active GitKraken Insights connection. See [Getting Started with GitKraken Insights](/gk-insights/getting-started) to enable Insights for your organization.
- Access to [GitKraken.dev](https://gitkraken.dev) with Insights enabled.

---

## Send a survey

Use the Survey Library to browse, preview, and distribute pre-built surveys to your team.

### 1. Open the Survey Library

In [GitKraken.dev](https://gitkraken.dev), go to **Insights > Surveys**. GitKraken Insights opens the **Survey Library** tab, which lists all available pre-built surveys.

### 2. Filter surveys by category

Select a category filter button at the top of the Survey Library to narrow the survey list:

- **All Surveys**
- **AI Adoption**
- **Development Tools**
- **Satisfaction & Sentiment**
- **Workflows and Processes**

Each survey card displays the survey title, description, category tag, question count, and estimated completion time.

### 3. Preview a survey (optional)

Click **Preview survey** on a survey card to review the survey questions before sending.

### 4. Open the send dialog

Click **Send survey** on the survey card you want to distribute. GitKraken Insights opens the **Send survey** modal.

### 5. Select recipients

In the **Send survey** modal:

1. Confirm the survey title and description.
2. Select recipients under **Teams** or **Individual users**.
3. Use **Select all teams** or **Select all users** to target your entire organization.

<figure>
  <img src="/wp-content/uploads/gk-insights-survey-dialog.png" class="help-center-img img-bordered" alt="Send survey dialog showing team and individual user recipient selection options" />
  <figcaption style="text-align: center; color: #888">Select recipients before sending a survey</figcaption>
</figure>

### 6. Send or schedule the survey

- Click **Send survey** to distribute the survey immediately.
- Click **Schedule** to set a future send date and time.

## View survey results

After GitKraken Insights sends a survey, responses appear in the **Survey Results** tab.

1. In [GitKraken.dev](https://gitkraken.dev), go to **Insights > Surveys**.
2. Click the **Survey Results** tab to view collected responses.

---

## Available surveys

GitKraken Insights includes pre-built surveys for the following topics:

- AI adoption
- CI/CD experience
- Code review process
- Developer experience
- Onboarding
- Team collaboration
- Technical debt
- Tooling satisfaction

## Troubleshooting

**The Surveys option is not visible in Insights**

GitKraken Insights requires an active connection to display the Surveys feature. See [Getting Started with GitKraken Insights](/gk-insights/getting-started) to enable Insights for your organization.
