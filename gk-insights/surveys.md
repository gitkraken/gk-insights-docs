## Overview

GitKraken Insights Surveys lets you send pre-built questionnaires to developers on your team. Survey results integrate with Insights metrics and industry benchmarks to surface actionable recommendations, combining code activity data with developer sentiment.

---

## Prerequisites

- An active GitKraken Insights connection. See [Getting Started with GitKraken Insights](/gk-insights/getting-started) to enable Insights for your organization.
- Access to [GitKraken.dev](https://gitkraken.dev) with Insights enabled.

---

## Send a survey

Use the Survey Library to browse, preview, and distribute pre-built surveys to your team.

### 1. Open the Survey Library

In [GitKraken.dev](https://gitkraken.dev), go to **Insights > Surveys**. GitKraken Insights opens the **Survey Library** tab, which lists all available pre-built surveys.

### 2. Filter surveys by category

Select a category filter button at the top of the Survey Library to narrow the survey list:

- **All Surveys**
- **AI Adoption**
- **Development Tools**
- **Satisfaction & Sentiment**
- **Workflows and Processes**

Each survey card displays the survey title, description, category tag, question count, and estimated completion time.

  <img src="/wp-content/uploads/gk-insights-surveys.png" class="help-center-img img-bordered" alt="GitKraken Insights Survey Library showing available pre-built surveys organized by category" />
  <figcaption style="text-align: center; color: #888">GitKraken Insights Survey Library</figcaption>
</figure>

### 3. Preview a survey (optional)

Click **Preview survey** on a survey card to review the survey questions before sending.

### 4. Open the send dialog

Click **Send survey** on the survey card you want to distribute. GitKraken Insights opens the **Send survey** modal.

### 5. Select recipients

In the **Send survey** modal:

1. Confirm the survey title and description.
2. Select recipients under **Teams** or **Individual users**.
3. Use **Select all teams** or **Select all users** to target your entire organization.

<figure>
  <img src="/wp-content/uploads/gk-insights-survey-dialog.png" class="help-center-img img-bordered" alt="Send survey dialog showing team and individual user recipient selection options" />
  <figcaption style="text-align: center; color: #888">Select recipients before sending a survey</figcaption>
</figure>

### 6. Send or schedule the survey

- Click **Send survey** to distribute the survey immediately.
- Click **Schedule** to set a future send date and time.

  <img src="/wp-content/uploads/gk-insights-schedule-survey-dialog.png" class="help-center-img img-bordered" alt="Schedule survey dialog for setting a future send date and time" />

## View survey results

After GitKraken Insights sends a survey, responses appear in the **Survey Results** tab. Use the tab to track response rates, review answers, and share survey links with recipients.

### 1. Open the Survey Results tab

1. In [GitKraken.dev](https://gitkraken.dev), go to **Insights > Surveys**.
2. Click the **Survey Results** tab.

GitKraken Insights displays all sent surveys. Each survey card shows the survey title, category tag, question count, estimated completion time, number of responses, and number of recipients.

<figure>
  <img src="/wp-content/uploads/gk-insights-surveys-results.png" class="help-center-img img-bordered" alt="Survey Results tab showing sent surveys with response counts and available actions" />
  <figcaption style="text-align: center; color: #888">Survey Results tab listing sent surveys and response data</figcaption>
</figure>

From each survey card, you can:

- Click **View results** to open the detailed results for that survey.
- Click **Copy link** to copy a direct link to the survey for manual distribution.
- Click **Send reminder** to send a follow-up notification to recipients who have not yet responded.

### 2. Review detailed results

Click **View results** on a survey card. GitKraken Insights opens the results view for that survey, which displays:

- **Overall score** — The average score across all responses.
- **Response rate** — The percentage of recipients who completed the survey.
- **Response rate trend** — The change in response rate over time.
- **Average score** — The mean score per question.

Each survey question appears as a bar chart showing the distribution of responses.

<figure>
  <img src="/wp-content/uploads/gk-insights-survey-results-view.png" class="help-center-img img-bordered" alt="Detailed survey results view showing overall score, response rate, and per-question bar charts" />
  <figcaption style="text-align: center; color: #888">Detailed survey results with per-question response distribution</figcaption>
</figure>

Click **Export** in the top-right corner to download the survey results data.

## Available surveys

GitKraken Insights includes pre-built surveys for the following topics:

- AI adoption
- CI/CD experience
- Code review process
- Developer experience
- Onboarding
- Team collaboration
- Technical debt
- Tooling satisfaction

## Troubleshooting

**The Surveys option is not visible in Insights**

GitKraken Insights requires an active connection to display the Surveys feature. See [Getting Started with GitKraken Insights](/gk-insights/getting-started) to enable Insights for your organization.
